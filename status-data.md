# Dexter status data

The status of the Dexter robot arm is returned to [DDE](DDE) by the [Firmware](Firmware) after every command.

It includes:
* DMA_READ_DATA
* DMA_READ_DATA
* RECORD_BLOCK_SIZE
* END_EFFECTOR_IO_IN
* BASE_POSITION_AT
* BASE_POSITION_DELTA
* BASE_POSITION_PID_DELTA
* BASE_POSITION_FORCE_DELTA
* BASE_SIN
* BASE_COS
* PLAYBACK_BASE_POSITION
* SENT_BASE_POSITION
* SLOPE_BASE_POSITION
